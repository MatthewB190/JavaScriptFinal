Controls and Moves

So The controls are basicly the same with the wii u and 3ds versions. A button for regular attack. B for speacial attacls. Up on the joystick(Wii U) or circle pad(3DS) will make you jump. R button or the right trigger on the 3DS or Wii U will shield you but be carful if the shield breaks it will stun you. and L button or trigger will grab your opponent. Now with the Smash Ball, You have to hit it. Once you hit it a few times, it will break and surround you in aura. Press B to unleash your Final Smash. Now you depending on your character you can be a certian range away from your opponent. So hit up training to learn about your character more, and just to train.
